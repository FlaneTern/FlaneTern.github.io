About Me

Probability theory, numerical optimization, machine Learning, game theory—you name it. I've always enjoyed exploring these areas in computer science. That is, until I found myself entangled (pun intended) in the quantum computing rabbit hole.

Do quantum speedups truly exist? If they do, what kinds of problems can quantum computers solve? Can we always find a classical algorithm that's only polynomially slower than its quantum counterpart? It would be fun if I could contribute to answering any of these questions.
